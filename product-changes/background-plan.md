### Background style creation steps

1. Create a single lilac flower
  a. color is a mix of purple and pink
  b. 4 petals in a cross section
  c. yellow dots in the middle of the flower known as stamens
  d. No need to worry about stems or leaves, only the flower
2. Create a variation of the first lilac flower
  a. same as step 1, but the petals should look slightly different in color and shape
  b. in the same group as the first flower
3. Create a variation of the first lilac flower
  a. same as step 1, but the petals should look slightly different in color and shape
  b. in the same group as the first flower
4. Create a group of 6 lilac flowers composed of the 3 flower variations.
  a. flowers can and should overlap each other somewhat to give a natural look
  b. this group should be called a flower bunch
  c. this component should be reusable
